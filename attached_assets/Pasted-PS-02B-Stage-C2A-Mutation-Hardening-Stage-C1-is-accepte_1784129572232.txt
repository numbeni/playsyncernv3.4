## 3. Prevent duplicate requests

Do not rely on `mutationKey` for duplicate-submit protection.

Add a synchronous ref-based lock or equivalent immediate guard to:

- Create submit
- Edit submit
- Status confirm

While pending:

- Submit or Confirm is disabled.
- Rapid repeated clicks cannot send another request.
- Escape cannot close the modal.
- Backdrop click cannot close it.
- Close and Cancel are disabled.
- The lock remains active during successful close animation.
- On failure, unlock so the user can retry.

Do not broadly refactor the modal system.
